Unofficial list of 3rd party boards support urls

29-37 minutes

Have you added Boards Manager installation support for an Arduino compatible board? Please let everyone know by publishing your package index URL.

List of 3rd party Boards Manager URLs

  • Adlink: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ADLINK/OT2IT/main/packages_adlink_ot2it.json

    • Adlink OT2IT Board, is a high-performance microcontroller that has a 32-bit ARM® Cortex®-M4 processor running at 120 MHz, 256 KB of SRAM and up to 1 MB Dual Panel Flash with ECC. A 10/100 Ethernet MAC and 2 CAN-FD ports provide connectivity for industrial automation, automotive, and general-purpose applications.

  • Adafruit: https://adafruit.github.io/arduino-board-index/package_adafruit_index.json

    • Adafruit AVR Boards (Flora, Metro, Trinket, Pro Trinket, & Gemma)

  • Adelino: http://adelino.cc/package_adelino_index.json

    • Adelino is a dual-microcontroller development board based on ATmega32U4 and ESP8266 (using ESP-12F WiFi module), inspired by Arduino Leonardo and Arduino Yún

  • AirMCU https://github.com/Air-duino/Arduino-pack-json-ci/releases/download/Nightly/package_air_index.json or https://arduino.luatos.com/package_air_cn_index.json (Users in China can use this link to avoid failing to install due to network factors)
